# 📊 Customer Retention & Churn Insights Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

The Customer Retention & Churn Insights Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze customer churn behavior and identify the key factors influencing customer retention. The dashboard provides business insights into customer demographics, contract types, internet services, tenure, payment methods, and churn rates to support data-driven customer retention strategies.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze customer churn patterns.
- Measure overall churn rate.
- Compare churn across different contract types.
- Analyze internet service usage.
- Study customer tenure and retention.
- Identify customer segments with high churn risk.

## 📈 Key Insights

- Overall churn rate is **19%**.
- Month-to-Month contracts have the highest churn.
- Fiber Optic customers show higher churn compared to other internet services.
- Customers with longer tenure are more likely to remain loyal.
- Female and Male customer distribution is nearly balanced.
- Contract type plays a significant role in customer retention.
